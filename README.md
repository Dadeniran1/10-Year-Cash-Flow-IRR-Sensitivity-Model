# 10-Year-Cash-Flow-IRR-Sensitivity-Model
## Objective: Modeled revenue growth, vacancy assumptions, operating expenses, debt financing, and exit valuation to evaluate investment performance. The model allows rapid assessment of rent increases, interest rate changes, and expense sensitivity, with outputs including NOI, CFBT, IRR, NPV, and sale proceeds.
## Tools Used: Ad Hoc Analysis, Microsfot Excel
## Business Questions Answered: What year will we break even? What vacany loss gives us a respectable NOI?
## Key Insights: $200/ month increase in rent per unit improves IRR by ~1.80 %. Cash flow breaks even by year 7
